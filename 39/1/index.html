Fastspeech2 with adverserial trainiong for emotional speech


Baseline model: Fastspeech2 + VAE + speaker and emotion embedding

Model 1: Weight of adversarial loss=0.3, Discriminator trained every 500 iterations, freeze encoder, duration predictor and two embedding, and Encoder, Decoder and Discriminator are conditioned on 2 embedding

Model 2: Weight of adversarial loss=0.7, Discriminator trained every 500 iterations, freeze post pitch predictor, encoder, duration predictor and two embedding, and Encoder, Decoder and Discriminator are conditioned on 2 embedding

Model 3: Weight of adversarial loss=0.7, Discriminator trained every 500 iterations, initilize and freeze only post pitch predictor, and Encoder, Decoder and Discriminator are conditioned on 2 embedding
