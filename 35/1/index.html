Model 1: Weight of adversarial loss=0.5, Discriminator trained every 200 iterations
Model 2: Weight of adversarial loss=0.5, Discriminator trained every 1 epoch, weights initilized, encoder freezed
Model 3: Weight of adversarial loss is adaptive with coefficient 0.01, , Discriminator trained every 200 iterations, Duration pridector is less complex and Pitch and duration loss *2 (Frame level)
